About Wildflour-Pâtisserie

I'm Jasmin, the blogger and baker behind Wildflour Pâtisserie. I have been a homemaker for many years and I love the French way of baking! That is why I decided to turn my life upside down, move to France and go to pastry school. While I'm there I want to learn as much as possible and I'm excited to share my experiences and knowledge with you!
